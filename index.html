Featured journey

Seven nights in Baja, with the kids.

  1. Day 1
    Arrive Los Cabos. Private driver up the Pacific coast to Todos Santos. Fresh agua fresca on the porch, kids chasing hummingbirds.
  2. Day 2
    Todos Santos, slow. Art galleries, Hotel California lunch, an afternoon at Cerritos for beginner surf swells.
  3. Day 3
    Desert to ocean. Morning horseback ride on empty beach, evening pastry crawl through the pueblo mágico.
  4. Day 4
    East to La Paz. Sea of Cortez sunset sail. Sea lions on the rocks, the kids perched on the bow.
  5. Day 5
    Whale sharks. Private panga and a marine biologist guide. Swim with the gentle giants (October through April).
  6. Day 6
    Lux Cabo. Transfer to a coastal resort on the Sea of Cortez. Pool day, beach bonfire, s’mores under the stars.
  7. Day 7
    Depart. Sportfishing at sunrise or spa at the resort. Farewell breakfast, hand-lettered itinerary to keep.
